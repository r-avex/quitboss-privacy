# Privacy Policy for QuitBoss

**Last updated: July 2, 2025**

QuitBoss ("the App") is developed by r-avex. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All personal data you enter (quit date, cigarettes per day, pack cost, game progress, achievements, and check-in history) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server, and we have no access to it.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

## Third-Party Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data:

- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- IP address (for approximate location)
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, video completions)

This data is collected by Google and may be used to serve personalized advertisements and track ad performance across apps. Google's use of this data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission before allowing AdMob to track your activity across other apps and websites. You may decline this request, and the App will continue to function normally with non-personalized ads.

## Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications (daily check-in reminders, milestone celebrations, and motivational messages). These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data (beyond what AdMob infers from IP)
- Health or fitness data
- Photos, files, or browsing history
- Any user-generated content

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
